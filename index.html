Benefits

Less Water

Many California native plants have adapted to dryer climates and have lower water requirements allowing homeowners to better manage water resources.

By using California native trees, plants, shrubs, and grasses and implementing other water-saving modifications, many property owners see their water usage reduced by up to 75%.

Easy Maintenance

Because native plants have adapted to their environment, you can expect to spend less time caring for them.

Native plants can thrive, and you can maintain your outside space in as little as a few hours each month.

Landscaping can increase your property value by as much as 10 to 12%.
